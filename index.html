Paradigm

Paradigm is a graphic design & curatorial endeavor that works as an accelerator for artists, artists-run spaces & new creative outlets.

Paradigm collaborates closely with creators in order to support them with branding & exhibition development, among other specific activities particular to each partnership. 

Projects

New Landscapes

Exhibition + Collaboration + Website + Outreach

We partnered with Florida State University to bring their New Landscapes exhibition to web. The exhibition features 30 projects from multiple artists exploring landscapes through photography and technology.⁣
